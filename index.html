About Me

I am an Experimental Perceptual-Motor Scientist with nearly a decade of experience using VR and AR to study human perception and locomotion. I received my PhD in Cognitive Science from Brown University and since Fall of 2020 I have been a post-doctoral researcher in the Human Movement Neuroscience Lab.

During my post-doc, I Co-Founded FreeMoCap. I'm the Chief Operations Officer of our Non-Profit, The FreeMoCap Foundation, where I lead in project management and championing usability. FreeMoCap takes modern advancements in computer vision and packages them to enable free 3D motion capture software for everyone!
