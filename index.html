About Me

I am a teaching professor at the University of Cincinnati where I'm spearheading technical education for PhD students in the Psychology Department.

In 2024, I founded the Visuo-Locomotor Neuroscience (ViLoN) Lab at UC.

During my post-doc working with Jon Matthis at Northeaster, I helped found The Free Motion Capture Project. I received my PhD in Cognitive Science from Brown University in the Winter of 2021 under the direction of Bill Warren.

In my free time, I enjoy playing games with my friends and hiking with my family.
