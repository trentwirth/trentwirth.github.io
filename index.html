About Me

I am a Computational Perceptual-Motor Scientist whose expertise is in the visual control of locomotion. My research has used Augmented and Virtual reality to investigate complex intra- and interpersonal human behavior in the traversal of complex terrain, and walking through crowds.

I am also supporting the usability and operations side of The Free Motion Capture Project (FreeMoCap | freemocap.org), making the recording and creation of human motion data easily accessible to animators and scientists.
